Outreach

Members of the VLND lab participate in various outreach programs every year to ensure community embeddedness. We participate in the following yearly events to both get the new generation excited about our research and to help raise funds for Alzheimer’s disease.

ASU Open Door at the Biodesign Institute

This annual event brings thousands of adults and children from the community to explore the ASU campus and participate in myriad interactive exhibits and activities showcasing the innovations that normally occur inside laboratories, classrooms, etc. The Velazquez lab volunteered in 2020 with brain hats, custom coloring postcards, and presentations on the lab’s research and Alzheimer’s disease in general.

Click to learn more about ASU Open Door

Walk to End Alzheimer’s – Alzheimer’s Association

The Alzheimer’s Association hosts this annual charity walk to raise awareness and funds for Alzheimer’s care, research and advocacy. In 2020 the Velazquez Lab (on behalf of the Biodesign NDRC) raised over $400 and lab team members participated in the walk virtually.
